Before you send your offer.

Clear answers about the free review, the 48-hour response, and what happens next.

Is the initial review really free?

Yes. There is no charge to submit your offer for an initial review. If a paid appraisal appears worthwhile, we explain the available report options before you decide.

What should I send?

Send the insurer’s offer amount, your vehicle’s year, make, model, mileage, and ZIP code. The insurer’s valuation report is strongly recommended because it shows the comparable vehicles and adjustments used.

What if my offer looks fair?

We’ll tell you. The free review is meant to help you decide whether further appraisal work appears worthwhile, not to sell a report in every case.

When will I hear back?

We respond within 48 hours after we receive the information needed to review your offer. If anything essential is missing or unreadable, we’ll email you with the specific item needed.

Does Spur Auto negotiate with the insurer for me?

No. Spur Auto provides independent valuation evidence and general response support, but does not contact or negotiate with the insurer on your behalf. You remain in control of all claim decisions and communications.
