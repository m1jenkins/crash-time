# Claim-value section concepts

Proposed insertion point: between the homepage case study and the existing
“From insurer offer to a clear next step” process section.

## Recommended: 01 — Build the case for more

This is the clearest bridge from the value-gap proof above it. It explains the
work in three concrete moves without suggesting that Spur Auto directly
negotiates with the insurer.

**Headline:** We build the case for more.

- Correct the details — Trim. Mileage. Options.
- Strengthen the evidence — Better local comparables.
- Document the higher value — A report built to push back.

Scope line: You stay in control. We give you the proof.

## Alternative: 02 — More value starts with better evidence

A calmer, more educational direction. It emphasizes evidence and leverage, but
the word “equip” is slightly less direct than the language in concept 01.

## Alternative: 03 — We help you push for more

The boldest, most energetic direction. Its short sequence—fix the facts, prove
the market, back your number—communicates advocacy quickly, but the full orange
band is a larger tonal departure from the current homepage.

### Refined 03 — Cream background

The refined version keeps concept 03's language and hierarchy while returning
to the homepage's warm cream canvas. Burnt orange is limited to the eyebrow,
short rule, and CTA, so the section feels assertive without becoming a red wall.

## Copy-only alternates

- We find the value they missed. Then we document it.
- A stronger number needs stronger evidence.
- Fix the facts. Prove the market. Back your number.
- We turn valuation gaps into a case for more.
- Your claim deserves more than the insurer's first draft.

These concepts intentionally avoid saying Spur Auto negotiates, contacts the
insurer, or guarantees a higher settlement.
