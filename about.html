The founder's story

Why I started Spur Auto

After an accident, my insurance company sent an offer that did not reflect what my vehicle was actually worth. Their comparison vehicles had problems that should have disqualified them: some carried salvage titles or accident histories, and some were listed at a price that did not match the dealer's real asking price. In one case, a report showed a dealer listing at $8,000 when the vehicle was actually offered for $10,000.

I was able to push back because I am an avid car enthusiast. I understand how the right mileage, trim level, and option packages affect a vehicle's value - and I know how to find a good comparable instead of taking the first number on a report at face value. That work helped me recover thousands of dollars more for my vehicle.

I started Spur Auto to share that knowledge with other drivers. Whether you are reviewing an insurance offer or shopping for a vehicle, our services are built to help you recognize the details that make a real difference in price.

- Mark West, Founder
