Know what happens next.

Direct answers to the questions that matter before you choose a report.

Which package is right for my total-loss claim?

Choose Essential for a standard-production vehicle. Choose Guided when you want help corresponding with the insurance company throughout negotiations, including personalized submission documents, response review, and a report walkthrough. Choose Specialty for a rare, modified, custom, or high-value vehicle that needs expanded research.

Are the report prices one-time fees?

Yes. Essential is $99, Guided is $299, Specialty is $299, and Specialty plus Guided is $449. There is no subscription or percentage of your settlement.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Standard delivery is within 48 hours after all required documents are received. Add 24-Hour Priority during Stripe Checkout for $49. If we miss that priority deadline, the $49 fee is refunded.

What if I need Guided and Specialty?

Purchase Specialty and add Guided Document Support for $150 during Stripe Checkout. Your total is $449 because the shared base report is charged only once. Adding 24-Hour Priority makes the total $498.

Does Guided include negotiation help?

Yes. With Guided, we help you throughout the negotiation process by reviewing insurance-company responses, explaining the issues, and helping draft your follow-up correspondence. You remain in control of the claim: you decide what to accept or reject and send every communication yourself. We do not contact the insurer or represent you as a public adjuster or attorney.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

If our independent appraisal does not support a value higher than your insurer’s offer, we refund the paid report fee in full.
