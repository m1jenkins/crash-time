Pricing questions

Know what happens next.

Direct answers to the questions that matter before you choose a report.

Which option is right for my claim?

Start free if you are unsure. Choose Total Loss Challenge when the insurer has declared your vehicle a total loss, or Diminished Value when a repaired vehicle is worth less because of its accident history.

Are the report prices one-time fees?

Yes. The $99 and $295 prices are one-time report fees with no subscription or percentage of your settlement. Optional negotiation coaching is a separate $195 flat fee.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Paid certified reports are delivered by email within 48 hours. The free claim snapshot arrives within two business days.

Do you need to inspect my vehicle in person?

No. The process is remote and document-based, using your photos, VIN, insurer valuation report, repair estimates, and market data.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

If our independent appraisal does not support a value higher than your insurer’s offer, we refund the paid report fee in full.

Not sure which report you need?

Start free. We will tell you honestly whether a paid report is worth it.
