Independent opinion of value

A Totaled Texas appraisal is an independent independent opinion of vehicle value based on the assignment, information supplied, market data reasonably available, stated assumptions, and effective date shown in the report. Vehicle markets change, listings may be removed or repriced, and reasonable appraisers may reach different conclusions. An appraisal is not a promise that an insurer, appraiser, umpire, lender, court, or other party will accept the concluded value.

No public-adjuster or representative relationship

Totaled Texas is an independent vehicle appraisal firm, not a public insurance adjuster.
