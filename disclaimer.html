Independent opinion of value

A Spur Auto appraisal is an independent opinion of vehicle value based on the assignment, information supplied, market data reasonably available, stated assumptions, and effective date shown in the report. Vehicle markets change, listings may be removed or repriced, and reasonable appraisers may reach different conclusions. An appraisal is not a promise that an insurer, appraiser, umpire, lender, court, or other party will accept the concluded value.

No public-adjuster or representative relationship

Spur Auto is an independent vehicle appraisal firm, not a public insurance adjuster. When Guided Document Support is purchased, we provide negotiation support and guidance by explaining appraisal findings, reviewing written insurer responses, and helping prepare draft correspondence throughout negotiations. We do not contact or negotiate with an insurer on a customer's behalf, settle a claim, make claim or coverage decisions, or act as the customer's representative. The customer decides what position to take, approves the content, and sends every communication.
